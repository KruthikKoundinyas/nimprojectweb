What to Expect

Optimal (XOR) vs Random: XOR wins ~90-95% when going first. It has a mathematical advantage from any non-zero Nim Sum position.

Optimal vs Q-Learning: Q-Learning should approach 40-50% wins with good training, but rarely beats a perfect player consistently.

Q-Learning vs Random: After training, Q-Learning typically wins 70-85%, demonstrating that it learned meaningful strategy.

Note: First-mover advantage exists in Nim. Half the games start each agent, so results reflect pure strategy quality.
